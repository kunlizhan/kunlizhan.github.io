As I write this, it is now about a half-year into the Great Pandemic of 2020. These days people are getting more comfortable with the pandemic lifestyle. But let's rewind a few months, back when western governments enacted their first lockdown policies, and the average person suddenly realized the magnitude of the changes coming for their lifestyles. For them, it seemed apocalyptic, as we suddenly started to categorize day-jobs as either "essential" or "non-essential".
